Welcome to Altitude Audio!

Who We Are

As an independent recording studio, Altitude Audio, sets its self apart from the others with their drive to produce the highest quality recordings for you. I take the recording process and make it more than just "studio time". With Austin Day (owner & engineer) your songs will not be done until they are exactly to your liking. I keep in mind that it is a stressful thing to be the artist and work with you to produce the hit songs & results that you want.

If you would like to record with Altitude Audio simply send us a message via email, or phone. We do have a set hourly rates and per-song rates, however, being a band member myself, I know it is hard to find a quality recording studio with prices that work with *YOU*. We will provide you with several payment options and even financing! So lets get started!
