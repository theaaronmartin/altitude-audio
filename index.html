Welcome To Altitude Audio!


Your new go-to studio in the Denver area for all of your recording, editing, mixing, mastering, photo & video needs. We have the drive and experience to help you get your song, your art, your heart, out into the world for others to enjoy. We offer very competitive pricing and a top notch product.

Along with audio recording we will also be offering video and photo services for those of you interested in play through videos (which is an awesome way to show your fans how to play along with their favorite songs) and session documentation. Session documentation will essentially be a photo/video album derived of your time at Altitude Audio. Packaged rates are available!

Our goal was to create a space that anyone would feel comfortable and creative in. Music is no easy job and I hope to make the recording process not only memorable, but something you can be proud of to show your friends, family, and most importantly your fans.

So give us call or drop me an email and let's get to work!

I look forward to meeting you all and giving Denver a new outlet for all the creativeness that this wonderful city has to offer!


-Austin Day

(Owner/Producer/Engineer)
